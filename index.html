Mount Kenya National Park

Facts and Interesting Places

There are many places in the world that have a beautiful scenery but none of these places beats the ambience that Nyeri offers. This county is located in the central highlands of Kenya. Its most appealing area is the Mount Kenya National Park. Here, you get to see amazing landscapes being grazed by animals such as the Mountain Bongo and the Zebras. The serene atmosphere in this park is perfect for a weekend getaway or a vacation and gives you an opportunity to unwind and reflect.

Here are some favorite places to visit:

  • Ragati Conservancy
  • Lake Michaelson
  • Lake Ellis
